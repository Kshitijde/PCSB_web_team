About Us

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) CSI Student Branch was established in 2016 with an objective to facilitate research, knowledge sharing learning and career enhancement for the students, while simultaneously inspiring and nurturing new entrants into the industry and helping them to integrate into the IT Community
Mission

  • Facilitate Research, Knowledge Sharing, Learning and Career Enhancement among all categories of IT professionals.
  • Inspire, nurture and assist students to integrate into the IT community.
  • Percolate benefits of IT advancement to all sections of Indian Society.

Vision

The Computer Society of India is a non-profit professional society and meet to exchange views and information learn and share ideas. The wide spectrum of members is committed to the advancement of theory and practice of Computer Engineering and Technology Systems, Science and Engineering, Information Processing and related Arts and Sciences.


PCSB works under Computer Society of india (CSI) to provide latest technology related problems to student.PCSB started with 147 students in academic year 2016-17.

PCSB arranges its flagship event Enthusia' every year around March.

PCSB works under the guidance of Dr.Kavita Sultanpure Ma'am, Prof.Kaustubh Sakhare Sir, Prof. Ashwini Jewalikar Ma'am.

Enthusia

  • Every year PCSB organizes Enthusia Fest. This event provides the students a platform to showcase their talents and augment their creativity.These competitions help students to improve their technical as well as non-technical events.

Enthusia Events

These are some of the technical competitions organized during the Enthusia fest.These competitions help students to showcase their techical skills and at the same time learn some new things.

Competetive Coding

Showcase your logical and coding skills in this straightforward coding competition. The task is simple: code to win!

Reverse Coding

If you think you have the skills to solve logical questions fast and find patterns, Reverse Coding is best for you.

Ninja Coding

Coding with a twist! Have a thrilling time with a messed-up keyboard, where you press one key, and something else gets typed.

Data Maestro

As the demand for data science skills is growing at lightning speed, we present you an ultimate Battle Ground of Data Scientists. Compete with others and showcase your data science skills to get a higher rank in the leader board.

Team

PCSB has various teams allocated for various sections.Every PCSB member gets to work in whichever team they desire. Every team work their their best to make any event a grand success.
